1. Defenition of Stream

-Streaming is transmitting digital audio or video content while users listen or watch. Material can be streamed from a private network or the public Internet, the latter now the streaming capital of the world. To the user, streaming is the same as listening to radio or watching cable, satellite or network TV, except that a connection to the Internet is required. Like network TV, streamed content may be regularly scheduled, or like Netflix and Hulu, content may be streamed on demand. It refers to any media content live or recorded that is delivered to computers and mobile devices via the internet and played back in real time with different genres and contents for each viewer. Often can be watched thru different websites (twitch, YouTube, FB gaming and more)

2. Types of Streaming Content

-There are music, video, tv or movies, live videos, and podcasts

3. Platforms used for streaming

-The most common streaming services are YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, netflix, amazon prime and more

4. Viewers analysis for each streaming category

Based from our research
~Demographics and behaviour - mostly teens, from all around the world and all genders and also have positive behavior
~Interest - poeple have different interest but when it comes to certain genre
~Engagement Metrics - based from our research with the count of viewsfrom likes and comments less than 40% interact with the stream in Youtube and Facebook, but in Twitch more than 50% of the viewers interact in chats

5. Need of viewers

-Based on our survey the most common need is that content should have a good quality and content that makes the audience or viewers stay and wait for more.

6. Characteristics of a good stream
